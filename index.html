MythGear Commons

Open, human-scale tools for movement, rest, warmth, and repair. Not products. Companions.

MythGear is a commons of practical, body-centered tools. Each one is designed to be repairable, adaptable, and rooted in lived human experience — not optimization, surveillance, or extraction.

These tools favor repair over replacement, learning over automation, dignity over control, and presence over performance. If a tool removes agency, it does not belong here.
