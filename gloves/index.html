Micro-listening sequence

  1. Place palm on bark, stone, metal, or ground.
  2. Soften the hand. Let weight settle.
  3. Wait for a temperature shift or faint vibration.
  4. Add 1–2 mm micro-movements.
  5. Notice changes in: buzz · density · echo · texture.
  6. Switch hands.
  7. Repeat on a new surface.

Design intent

You started with “32 points/joints + wrists.” This build compresses complexity into a smaller set of reliable “listening moves” — reduce to ~12 and make them fine-tunable.

Core Architecture

Palm · resonance cup

Shallow concavity (target 3–5 mm) that “hears” vibration and returns a note through the hand. Optional porous jute lining. Optional removable disk.

Outer palm · jute & grit

Grip + abrasion layer with flex channels aligned to palm lines for micro-movement fidelity.

Back of hand · breathable

Woven textile. Optional “pulse lane” (future CodeTape integration).

Closure · strap system

Field-repairable. Candidates: velcro / button / “claw” tab. (Notes: “straps like the ones from Japan.”)

Reminder
Hands are not gripping. Hands are listening.

Movement Map

The glove is not only “hand.” It is a pathway that can climb: wrist → elbow → shoulder (and even to waist) so micro-contact becomes whole-body calibration.

Reduce to ~12 micro-moves

  • Press / release
  • Feather slide (1–2 mm)
  • Circle (micro)
  • Rocking (heel of palm ↔ fingers)
  • Twist (soft)
  • Tap (stone-tap variant)
  • Hover (near-contact)
  • Pulse lane trace (future)

We can refine the “official 12” as you test.

Why the reduction matters

Fewer moves = more repeatable listening, less performance noise. The glove becomes a training instrument, not a “tech glove.”

Palm Pad Questions

From your notes: palm pad material + size. These are v1 decision points.

Materials to test

  • Coconut coir layer (texture + breath)
  • Bamboo thin disk (crisp “note”)
  • Carbon fiber (sharp response, but watch comfort + story-fit)
  • Wood (birch/bamboo) sealed (warm resonance)
  • Silicone (soft damping option)

Size ranges

  • Small cup: ~18–22 mm
  • Medium cup: ~22–28 mm
  • Large cup: ~28–34 mm
