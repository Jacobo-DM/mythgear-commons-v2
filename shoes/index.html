Shoes

Resonance-plate midfoot for ground-contact awareness. Feet that remember where they've been.

MythGear Shoes are minimalist movement shoes designed for RageDance, trail wandering, and urban stealth. They emphasize ground feel over cushioning, clean lines over bulk, and feedback over numbness.

The core feature is a continuous Achilles rail that wraps heel to sides to toe, paired with a resonance plate at the midfoot — transmitting contact information upward rather than absorbing and erasing it.
