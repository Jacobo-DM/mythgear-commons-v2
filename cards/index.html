Cards

Small artifacts that shift attention rather than command it. Three sequences, three entry points into the MythGear practice.

Each card is a short somatic sequence — a way in. They work alone, in pairs, or in a group. They work before a session or as the whole session. Print them, copy them, hand them to someone who needs one.

Cards are paired with Gloves and Shoes as part of the MythGear Set, but they don't require any equipment. Just hands, feet, and attention.

Hands Listen

Micro-listening sequence. Palm on bark, stone, metal, or ground. Soften. Wait. Notice temperature shift and faint vibration.

Open card ↗

Feet Remember

Echo-walking sequence. Slow steps on any surface. Feel resonance at midfoot. The feet are recording; let them.

Open card ↗

Pulse Bridge

Hands and feet integration. Trace slow infinity circles. Breathe into the back body. Connect the two listening surfaces.

Open card ↗

How to Use

Each card is a plain Markdown file — easy to print, copy, adapt, or translate. The sequences are short by design. The point is not completion; it is arrival.

See also: Field Protocol v1 — the full three-part MythGear practice that these cards distill.
